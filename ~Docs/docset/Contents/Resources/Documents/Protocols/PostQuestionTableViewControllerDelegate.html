PostQuestionTableViewControllerDelegate Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in PostQuestionTableViewController.h

Overview

It’s a delegate used to monitor the status of the question being posted.
It will get called in NewestQueryTableViewController, in order to track the status of the post request.

– postQuestionWithQuestionObject:notificationTitle:message:image:error: required method
